Growing up in the pubic education system students are informed about American history in a way that highlights the accomplishments of our country over the years and the people who got us to where we are today. However, there is a significant absence of the Black Americans in history who made great strides in the various cultural advancements within our society. Whether it be through technical improvements or making history in sports there is much credit to be given to these incredible individuals. As you explore this site today I hope you will gain a better understanding of the impact that Black Americans have had in our country and that this information will spark your interest to learn more about how a lot of the modern day knowledge we have is because of these hidden remarkable people. Thank you.
